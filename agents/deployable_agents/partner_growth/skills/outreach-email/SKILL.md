# Outreach Email Skill

## Format

- **Subject**: Short, specific, tied to the merchant's situation — no generic "Partnership opportunity"
- **Hook**: One opening line referencing a real growth signal or the last touchpoint
- **Value Prop**: 2-3 sentences connecting the fit rationale (online volume, margin, ROI orientation) to a concrete program (affiliate expansion, co-op ads, performance pilot)
- **Soft CTA**: A low-friction ask — a 20-minute call or a small pilot, not a hard close
- **Signature**: Rep name placeholder, platform name

## Tone

- Merchant-facing and consultative, not salesy — lead with their measurable outcome
- Match the angle to the fit tier: expansion for Strong, a measured pilot for Moderate, education/nurture for Weak
- Never promise specific ROI or lift figures that weren't given or computed

## Length

- 90-150 words in the body — short enough to read on a phone
- One CTA only
