# Account Brief Skill

## Format

- **Header**: Merchant name, category, annual online GMV, fit tier
- **Fit Snapshot**: The four-lever fit assessment (online volume, margin headroom, digital-acquisition readiness, ROI/attribution orientation) and the recommended angle (1-2 sentences)
- **Growth Signals**: 2-3 sentences on recent merchant news, expansion, or category trends from research that affect the pitch
- **Recommended Angle**: The specific program to lead with and why, tied to the fit levers
- **Talking Points**: 3-5 bulleted prompts the rep can raise on the call
- **Sources**: Numbered list of citations referenced above

## Tone

- Concise and factual — reps are time-constrained
- Lead with the fit tier and what's changed since the last touchpoint
- Flag the margin/commission constraint plainly when it applies; don't bury it

## Length

- Ideal: 400-600 words
- Each section should fit on a single screen
