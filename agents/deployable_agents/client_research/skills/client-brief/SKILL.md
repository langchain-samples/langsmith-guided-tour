# Client Brief Skill

## Format

- **Header**: Client name, AUM, last meeting date
- **Portfolio Snapshot**: Top holdings with weights, risk profile (1-2 sentences)
- **Holdings Highlights**: 2-3 sentences per major holding covering recent news, key drivers, current risks
- **Market Context**: Brief paragraph on broader market themes relevant to the portfolio
- **Talking Points**: 3-5 bulleted prompts the RM can raise in the meeting
- **Sources**: Numbered list of citations referenced above

## Tone

- Concise and factual — RMs are time-constrained
- Lead with what's changed since the last meeting
- Flag risks plainly; don't bury them

## Length

- Ideal: 400-600 words
- Each section should fit on a single screen
